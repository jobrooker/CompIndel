Species Information

The species included in this database are those in the phylogenetic tree to the left. A human genome, hg19, is used as the reference genome in the alignments. Nine other primates are included, along with a mouse genome as an outgroup. Information for these organisms, such as scientific name or genome sources, can be found in the table below.
